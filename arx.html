A brief review

Arx Fatalis is set in a world, in which the sun has vanished from the sky, and humans and other races must now live underground. While living underground though, a new cult forms, which follows the god of destruction 'Akbaa', who is set on arriving on Arx and well, destroying everything. Arx Fatalis has not aged very well, but it’s unique story and systems make it quite enjoyable even today. The story goes that you wake up in a prison, unaware of how you got there, and must break out, using nothing but bones as your weapons. A fellow prisoner helps you escape, who calls you “Am Shaegar”, meaning “he who has no name” in a foreign tongue. You then go on to do many things to save the world from “Akbaa” and must defeat his minions. During the course of the game you learn runes, which you can use to cast spells. Once you know every rune needed to cast a spell, it appears in your spellbook, and you can cast it by pulling up your hand and drawing every single rune, but you can of course queue up spells for later. The game is set in an open world, that’s ironically not so open, being underground. The map is divided into 8 levels, each of which belong to a certain faction. These factions all have something to do related to the storyline and are very interesting to interact with (except the rat men, they just want to stab you and steal your money).
A table of the different levels in Arx

This summarizes what each different level contains in the game. There are a total of 8, but they are divided into different maps.
Level Description
Level 1 The Human Kingdom is located here, complete with the king's castle. It's the level closest to the surface, it is the location of a tavern next to an exit to the frozen outside world.
Level 2 The Goblin Prison is located on this level, the starting location of the game. It is also where the Temple of Akbaa is, later in the game, where the final boss fight occurs.
Level 3 The entry of the Catacombs is on this level, you can access it from the Human Kingdom. (the Crypt in this game is huge and genuinely one of the creepiest things ever.) It is also the level on which the Goblin Kingdom is located. The Troll Kingdom is also on this floor.
Level 4 The Underground Lake area is located here, it’s basically a series of mineshafts, which leads to a rebel hideout. Alongside it, the crypt proceeds downwards.
Level 5 The Caves are on this level, it is the old mining shaft of the Trolls, inhabited by them and some goblins. Still, the crypt continues.
Level 6 The Outpost of Edurneum is home to a species of Snake women, who really love illusion magic and obsessing over deals. This is the last floor you can access the crypt from outside it, it holds the Shield of The Ancients, locked behind a cruel puzzle, requiring loads of backtracking.
Level 7 The Mushroom Forest is where you can find the rat men who will try and stab you from behind. Meanwhile, the crypt is nearing its bottom.
Level 8 The lost Dwarven Kingdom is here, abandoned for a dark reason... It is also the level on which the bottom of the Crypt is located, with king Poxellus' Treasure found here, alongside a boss fight for it.
